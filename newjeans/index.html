ABOUT

NewJeans (뉴진스) is a 5-member girl group under ADOR and HYBE Labels. The members consist of Minji,
Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein. They released their debut single “Attention” on July 22, 2022,
followed by their debut extended play, New Jeans, which was released on August 1, 2022.

Facts

- NewJeans is the first girl group under ADOR, a subsidiary label of HYBE that focuses on producing pop music.
- NewJeans has a diverse lineup of members from different backgrounds and nationalities. Minji and Haerin are Korean, Hanni is Vietnamese-Australian, Danielle is Korean-Australian, and Hyein is Korean-Vietnamese.
- NewJeans debuted with the single "Attention", which topped the Circle Digital Chart for four consecutive weeks and became one of the best-selling songs of 2022 in South Korea.
- NewJeans is known for their girl next door image and their retro-inspired pop and R&B songs that pay homage to the music of the 1990s.
- NewJeans has a fandom name called Bunnies (Tokki/토끼), which was chosen by the members themselves. The name reflects their cute and playful personalities and their love for their fans.
- NewJeans has collaborated with several famous brands, such as Gucci, Levi's, Coca-Cola, LG Electronics, and McDonald's Korea. They have also been featured in magazines such as Vogue, Elle, and Cosmopolitan.
- NewJeans has won several awards since their debut, including Rookie of the Year at the Mnet Asian Music Awards, Golden Disc Awards, Melon Music Awards, and Seoul Music Awards. They have also been named as one of Time's Next Generation Leaders and Forbes's Korea Power Celebrity 40.
- NewJeans has a close relationship with BTS, another group under HYBE. Minji and Hanni appeared in BTS's music video for "Permission to Dance", and BTS has shown their support for NewJeans on various occasions.
